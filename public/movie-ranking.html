Best Movies of All Time

Following are some Top 3 Movies According to me:

Journey to the Center of the Earth

Journey to the Center of the Earth* follows a daring expedition to the Earth's core, where a group of explorers discovers a world of prehistoric creatures, natural wonders, and unimaginable dangers.

OZ: The Great and Powerful

Oz the Great and Powerful* tells the origin story of a small-time circus magician who is transported to the magical land of Oz, where he must confront dark forces and fulfill his destiny as the legendary Wizard.

Around the World in 80 Days

Around the World in 80 Days* follows the adventurous Phileas Fogg as he attempts to circumnavigate the globe in just 80 days, facing thrilling challenges and unexpected encounters along the way.
